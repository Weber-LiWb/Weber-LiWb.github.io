Research

6 projects · by theme

Aligning LLMs to platform economics

Theme 01 · ongoing
Post-training agents for platform-level objectives
Most post-training optimizes proximal signals — helpfulness, refusal, single-turn preference. What happens when the objective we actually care about is retention, welfare, or market efficiency? Exploring preference-based fine-tuning as a bridge.
post-training · platforms · in progress
Reward shaping for agentic workflows
Which post-training signals actually transfer to multi-step tool use and delegation? Early evidence that naive preference data is worse than useless for agents.
agents · draft
