This is Weibo Li (he/him). I'm a second-year PhD student in Computer Information Systems at the W.P. Carey School of Business, Arizona State University.

I earned my bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems from Tongji University, where I had the honor of working as a research assistant under Associate Professor Xiaofeng Ma on topics related to Blockchain and its applications in Finance. Additionally, I was fortunate to be supervised by Professor Xiaolei Wang on my first research project in shared mobility, as well as on my bachelor’s thesis.

My research interests span a wide range of areas, with a particular focus on leveraging AI technology in business responsibly and sustainably (socially and economically).
