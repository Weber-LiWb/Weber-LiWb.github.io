Research Interests

My research interests span a wide range of areas, with a particular focus on leveraging AI technology in business responsibly and sustainably (socially and economically).

Selected Projects

Evaluating Ride-sharing Matching Strategies: Impacts on User Satisfaction and Efficiency

Investigating how different matching strategies affect the user experience of ride-sharing services through systematic simulation.

Enhancing Ride-sharing Services: An Experiment Framework Combining Prediction and Simulation Modules

Enhance the practicality of a prediction model by improving computing efficiency and the accuracy of the upgraded prediction model through simulation. Aiming to provide operational insights by analyzing simulation results thereby improving the practice of the prediction model.

Deep Learning-based Identification System of Small Orbital Remnants

A system was designed to autonomously detect small orbital remnants (less than 10*10 pixels on the image) through unmanned patrol vehicles and send warnings accordingly. The detection system was based on Yolo V3.

Meta-learning and Hyperparameter Optimization based on Double-layer Programming Structure

Combined meta-learning and Hyperparameter Optimization via a double-layer programming structure. Verified that the approach of ‘approximate gradient’ can greatly reduce the computational time complexity.
